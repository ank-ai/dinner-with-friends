Hotpot



Hotpot is a dish, that is often eaten in China. Preparation is the key.

Usually, there are bowls of different meats, fish, vegetables, tofu, sauces and other foods prepared, which can be put in the soup depending on what you feel like eating.

Everyone can put food into the soup and eat whatever they feel like eating, which is very versatile.


ingredients

  • Hotpot soup base (found at asian supermarkets)
  • vegetables: e.g. pak choi, mushrooms, potato...
  • thinly sliced meats: e.g. pork, beef or lamb
  • different tofu types
  • ramen noodles


steps

  1. prepare all the ingredients into cookable sizes
  2. boil water in a pot with the hotpot base
  3. once the water is boiling, you can add whatever you feel like eating


additional information

You should use a portable gas stove, otherwise you would have to stay near your stove the entire time.

I would recomment you to visit a hotpot restaurant first before attempting to hotpot at home, to get a feel for how it works
