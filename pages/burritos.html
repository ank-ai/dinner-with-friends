Burritos



A burrito is a dish in Mexican and Tex-Mex cuisine that took form in Mexican cuisine,

rconsisting of a flour tortilla wrapped into a sealed cylindrical shape around various ingredients.


ingredients

  • onion (finely chopped)

  • tortillas
  • rice
  • avocado

steps

  1. fry onions
  2. cook beans and rice
  3. cook meat

  5. wrap it up
