Lasagna



Lasagna, is a type of pasta made of very wide, flat sheets.

The dish is made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings

such as ground meats and tomato sauce, vegetables, cheeses and seasonings and spices.


ingredients

  • minced meat
  • onion and garlic
  • tomato products e.g. cans of: crushed tomatoes, tomatosauce, tomato paste
  • spices and seasonings: e.g. parsley, basil, salt, balck pepper
  • cheeses e.g. parmesan, moozzarella, ricotta
  • lasagna pasta

steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. cook the noodles
  3. make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. bake
  6. let the lasagna rest before serving
